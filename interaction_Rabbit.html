KUKU’S DAILY ROUTE

Kuku’s Daily Route is an interactive game about modern life, fleeting moments, and introspection. Kuku’s life is a tightly scheduled whirlwind of tasks—her days divided into checkpoints she must reach on time. As you navigate her routine, Kuku’s Daily Route serves as a commentary on the pace of modern life and how easily we overlook the emotional value of everyday encounters. By juxtaposing a hurried experience with a reflective journey, the project nudges players to reevaluate their priorities and embrace moments of connection amid the chaos of routine.

Each task feels like a simple stop: the gym, grabbing a meal, heading to work—but every action either deepens her connections or leaves them shallow. The faster you rush, the less Kuku feels, and the more automated her day becomes.

Play seated with a mouse and keyboard or an optional controller.

Kuku’s Daily Route can be presented on PC. Take your time—if you dare.
